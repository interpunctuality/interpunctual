abstract

the origins of this configuration trace back to an insightful observation made professor hal tussig, who suggested a potential connection between performance art and the origins of “thunder, perfect mind,” a famous poem found among the nag hammadi library documents. informed by this perspective, i developed a generative ambient patch to accompany creative, meditative, and ecstatic audible readings of the text.
