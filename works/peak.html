abstract

conceived in the first apartment i lived in alone and named after the street it was on, >peak< comprehensively exemplifies my early attempts to develop a distinctive, cohesive musical aesthetic. energetic vocals nestled between a rumbling sub-bass countermelody and an ethereal, high-frequency lead fill the aural spectrum, accompanied by icy synth stabs. grounded by pounding trap drums, this track features lyrics that describe an ontology of immanence through the framework of psychedelic experience and artwork that reflects these themes.
